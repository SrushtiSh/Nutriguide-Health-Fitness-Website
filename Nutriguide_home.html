NutriGuide has an intelligent method for promoting a healthy lifestyle for those who are having difficulty maintaining a healthy weight.


Maintaining healthy weight

A healthy weight, or normal weight, means that your Body Mass Index falls within a weight range that is not associated with an increased risk for weight-related diseases and health issues. Body Mass Index is determined by your height and your weight.A higher BMI tends to indicate higher body fat, and a lower BMI indicates lower body fat.
